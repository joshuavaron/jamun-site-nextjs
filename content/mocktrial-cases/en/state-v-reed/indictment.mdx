**THE GRAND JURY OF THE COUNTY OF NORWOOD, by this indictment, accuses PEYTON REED of the following offenses:**

## Count I — Murder in the First Degree

The defendant, **PEYTON REED**, on or about the 24th day of April, 2025, in the County of Norwood, State of Highfield, did **purposefully and knowingly** cause the death of another person, to wit, **WEST BAILEY**, by means of **poison**.

The defendant, knowing that West Bailey was severely allergic to pineapple extract, did introduce pineapple extract into a beverage prepared for and consumed by West Bailey, causing a fatal allergic reaction and the death of West Bailey.

The foregoing acts were committed in violation of **18 HPC § 303(A) (Murder in the First Degree)**.

## Count II — Murder in the Second Degree

The defendant, **PEYTON REED**, on or about the same date and in the same county, did **recklessly** cause the death of West Bailey by administering pineapple extract into a beverage consumed by West Bailey.

The foregoing acts were committed in violation of **18 HPC § 303(B) (Murder in the Second Degree)**.

---

The Grand Jury of the County of Norwood, having heard the evidence presented and having found reasonable cause to believe that the defendant committed the offenses charged, returns this indictment and holds the defendant, **PEYTON REED**, to answer.

To each count, the defendant has entered a plea of **NOT GUILTY**.

A TRUE BILL.
