Four high-school friends — Jordan Varon, Drew Simon, West Bailey, and Peyton Reed — built the prank channel **Apex** into a 1.2-million-subscriber phenomenon in a matter of months, dropped out of school, and moved into a shared content house they called **The Ridge**. Fame came fast, and so did conflict: the group fought bitterly over the channel's creative direction and over how its money and control were split.

On the night of **April 24, 2025**, West Bailey drank from the group's nightly tea and fell asleep on the living-room couch. The next morning Bailey was found unresponsive and was pronounced dead. The cause of death was **pineapple extract** — a substance to which Bailey was severely allergic — found in Bailey's tea cup.

The State of Highfield alleges that Peyton Reed, who stood to lose control of the channel in an imminent vote, deliberately poisoned West Bailey. Reed has pleaded not guilty.
