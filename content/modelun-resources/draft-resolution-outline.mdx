## Header Information

### Title
Be creative!

*Example: TGIF (Tackling Global Illicit Finance)*

### Committee Name
*Example: United Nations Security Council*

### Sponsors
Main authors.

*Example: China, Russia, France, Algeria, etc.*

### Signatories
Nations that want the paper to be brought to the floor to debate.

*Example: Sierra Leone, Switzerland, Malta*

### Topic
*Example: The Russo-Ukrainian War*

## Preambulatory Clauses

### Beginning
A verb in the present participle (Recognizing, Noting, etc.)

### Content
Background information, the problem, and past efforts to address it

### Ending
End with a comma.

*Example: Recognizing the efforts of countries, humanitarian organizations, and the United Nations in providing aid, shelter, and medical care to those affected by the war,*

## Operative Clauses

### Beginning
A verb in the third person present (Calls, Seeks, Urges, etc.)

### Content
The sponsors' proposed solutions: funding, subcommittees, other strategies

### Note
UN committees (except UNSC) can't force action, so use verbs like Requests, Encourages, etc.

### Ending
End each clause with a semicolon (except the final clause, which ends with a period).

*Example: Enacts an immediate ceasefire between Russia and Ukraine;*

## Quick Reference

| Element | Description | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Title | Creative acronym or name | TGIF |
| Committee | Your committee name | UN Security Council |
| Sponsors | Main authors (max 5% of committee) | China, Russia, France |
| Signatories | Want it debated (min 20% of committee) | Sierra Leone, Switzerland, Malta |
| Topic | The resolution topic | The Russo-Ukrainian War |
| Preambulatory | Background, context (end with comma) | *Recognizing...*, |
| Operative | Action items (end with semicolon) | *Calls upon...*; |
