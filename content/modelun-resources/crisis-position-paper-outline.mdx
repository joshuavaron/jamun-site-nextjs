## Introduction

- Who are you (name, occupation, setting)?
- What about you is important (family, wealth, title, etc.)?

## Paragraph #1: Committee (Frontroom)

### Topic Sentence
What will your character's interests be during committee? How will you act in front of your peers? What are your public goals?

### Context
What can you do about it? How do your portfolio powers empower you to achieve your goals?

### Evidence
What are the specific details from your life that grant you the power to do what you would like to do?

### Analysis
How will achieving your goals affect your character? Why do you want to achieve these goals? How will you use your portfolio powers to accomplish them?

### Concluding Sentence
How do your goals contribute to the whole committee?

## Paragraph #2: Personal (Backroom)

### Topic Sentence
What will your character attempt to do in the backroom? How will your character use crisis notes to achieve their private interests?

### Context
How will your private goals impact your powers?

### Evidence
What are the specific details from your life that grant you the power to do what you would like to do?

### Analysis
How will achieving your goals affect your character? Why do you want to achieve these goals? How will you use your portfolio powers to accomplish them?

### Concluding Sentence
How will your goals affect your character as a whole?

## Conclusion

- What are the two or three most important things you will try to do during the committee?
- How do your skills and qualities enable you to act on these goals?
