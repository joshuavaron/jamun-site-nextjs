## Header Information

### Title
Be creative!

*Example: Developing Unforgettable Simulations for Thoughtful International Negotiations (DUSTIN)*

### Committee Name
*Example: JAMUN Secretariat*

### Sponsors
Main authors.

*Example: Dustin, Joshua, Emma*

### Signatories
Delegates that want the directive to be brought to the floor to debate.

*Example: Sahana, Sam, Miranda, Abby*

## Operative Clauses

### Beginning
A verb in the simple present (Direct, Move, Create, etc.)

### Content
The sponsors' proposed solutions such as:
- Obtaining funding
- Building an army
- Creating new institutions
- Etc.

### Ending
End each clause with a period.

*Example: Create a national university to educate Canadian citizens in Law and Economics.*
